We Are

Organic Harvests Digital (OHD)- an environment action and community reinvestment organization. We focus on adapting climate hardware and internet of things (IoT) technologies to solve the issues of hunger, food sustainability, while promoting equity in farming and access to digital technologies.

OHD

By the end of the century, the UN expects a global population of 11.2 billion. As global health becomes better and mortality decreases, these factors place greater pressure onto cultivating land and the food production system.

It becomes critical to begin a new agricultural revolution where the decarbonizing farmlands, promoting sustainability, and prioritizing scalability with affordability are the fundamental themes driving this development.

OHD is centered on supporting climate-vulnerable communities. In helping people and communities that are in food deserts tracts, and who are food insecure, OHD provides an Eco-friendly, modular, agricultural system that can grow fresh, organic leafy and microgreens.

Leafy and Microgreens

The Indoor Farming System (IFS) is a holistic food-water-energy system that is capable of growing fresh, organic, leafy and microgreens year-round!

Community Education

OHD offers a climate, experiential program designed to create a pipeline of informed climate citizens.
