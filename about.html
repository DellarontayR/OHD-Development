Where Feeding People Matters

Our mission is focused on feeding people. Our business believes that access to quality and nutritious food is essential for human physical and mental wellness. It is also fundamental for the environment and advancing sustainable agriculture. We understand the broad interdependent relationship between food, social factors, economic benefits, improving education, health outcomes, and many other developmental goals. As a result, we are committed to supporting the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals.

Sustainable Agriculture

Sustainable agriculture is a new farming solution essential to building a healthy, vibrant, and thriving planet for all. The use of vertical-indoor farming and other innovative food-growing technologies allows us to grow nutritious food while minimizing the need for land needs, negative impact on the environment, and input resources. With a focus on reducing carbon footprint, promoting biodiversity, and increasing food production, sustainable farming will ensure a better life and brighter future for generations

Vertical Farming

The future of farming is vertical-indoor farming. OHD's vertical indoor farming system allows crop-production year round, uses less water, fewer pesticides all while maximizing crop yield. The mission of vertical-indooring farming is in building local and sustainable food systems.
At a time when our planet is facing extreme environmental challenges, the innovative solutions of vertical-indoor farming are critical to feeding a growing population

Farm to Table

At Organic Harvest Digital Inc. (OHD), we believe in the power of farm-to-table. Food should be fresh, healthy, inexpensive, and sustainable. With our vertical-indoor farming system, we grow and harvest high-quality, premium leafy greens and produce locally. By reducing the time from harvest to plate, we can preserve the fresh and rich flavors to deliver to you. Join us in our mission as we celebrate local produce, sustainable farm practices, and everything farm-to-table
